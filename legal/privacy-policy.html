Privacy Policy for 2048 Gamer

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Trung Tang ("we," "us," or "our") provides the 2048 Gamer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Handle

Information stored locally

The App stores current game state, best score, score history and timestamps, selected themes, and onboarding status locally on your device. This data is not intentionally transmitted to us.

Information you provide

If you contact us through the App's feedback feature, your email provider may process your email address, message, and any information you choose to include. We use it only to respond and provide support.

Technical information

The App includes platform and third-party software components. If diagnostic services are enabled in a future release, they may process limited technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and diagnostic data to maintain and improve the App.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain gameplay features
  • Save preferences and game progress
  • Respond to feedback and support requests
  • Diagnose problems and improve reliability
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Advertising, Accounts, and Purchases

The current App does not provide user accounts, contain third-party advertising, offer in-app purchases, or intentionally collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or financial information.

4. Third-Party Services

When you choose to rate the App or send feedback, the App may open an external service such as Google Play or your email provider. Those services handle information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Game data is retained locally until you clear it through available App features, clear App storage, or uninstall the App. Support communications are retained only as long as reasonably necessary. The App does not provide accounts or maintain a user database.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information, but no device, storage system, or electronic communication method can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-audience puzzle game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe a child submitted personal information through a support request.

8. International Users

Information submitted through an external service may be processed in other countries according to that service provider's terms and privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App or applicable requirements change. We will update the effective date when changes are made.

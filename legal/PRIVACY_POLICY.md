# Privacy Policy for 2048 Gamer

**Effective date: June 10, 2026**

Trung Tang ("we," "us," or "our") provides the **2048 Gamer** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Handle

### Information stored locally

The App stores the following information locally on your device so that its features work:

- Current game state and best score
- Score history and timestamps
- Selected backgrounds and tile themes
- Onboarding completion status

This local game data is not intentionally transmitted to us. It remains on your device unless you choose to share information through another app or service.

### Information you provide

If you contact us through the App's feedback feature, your email provider may process your email address, message, and any other information you choose to include. We use this information only to respond to your request and provide support.

### Technical information

The App includes platform and third-party software components required to provide app functionality. If diagnostic services are enabled in a future release, they may process limited technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and diagnostic data. This information would be used only to maintain and improve the App.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

- Provide and maintain gameplay features
- Save your preferences and game progress
- Respond to feedback and support requests
- Diagnose problems and improve reliability
- Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

## 3. Advertising, Accounts, and Purchases

The current version of the App:

- Does not require or provide user accounts
- Does not contain third-party advertising
- Does not offer in-app purchases
- Does not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or financial information

## 4. Third-Party Services

When you choose to rate the App or send feedback, the App may open an external service such as Google Play or your email provider. Those services handle information according to their own privacy policies.

The App may include software libraries supplied by platform or service providers. We are responsible for ensuring that any enabled third-party services are used consistently with this Privacy Policy and applicable store requirements.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Game progress, preferences, and score history are retained locally until you clear them through available App features, clear the App's storage, or uninstall the App.

Support communications are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain support records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

Because the App does not provide accounts or maintain a user database, there is no separate account deletion process.

## 6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information. However, no device, storage system, or method of electronic communication can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-audience puzzle game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, please contact us so that we can review and delete it where appropriate.

## 8. International Users

If information is submitted through an external service, it may be processed in countries other than your own according to that service provider's terms and privacy practices.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App or applicable requirements change. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
